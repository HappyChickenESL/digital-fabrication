Digital Chess Board

My final project idea is a digital Chess Board.
I have two nieces who enjoy playing chess but still have a hard time.
I think it would be a cool idea since I also like chess.
The basic idea is to first create a Chess Board, ideally with all figures
Each tile of the chess board would need to be wired to sense the figure that is standing on top of it
When the figure is picked up, the tiles which the figure can move to, are highlighted
Furthermore, tiles which are controlled by an enemy unit, could be highlighted red
A timer could be integrated if you want to play blitz chess with a timer
